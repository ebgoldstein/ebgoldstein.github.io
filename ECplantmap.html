An interactive map of literature studies of Uniola paniculata and Ammophila breviligulata along the US east coast

The focus of this map is the region of range overlap for these two species (NJ-NC).

This map accompanies a paper that is currently in review.

  • Orange dots — locations of studies focusing on Ammophila breviligulata.
  • Blue dots — locations of studies focusing on Uniola paniculata.
  • Purple dots — locations where both are studied.

    • The map is interactive, you can zoom, pan, and click markers.

    Clicking on a marker will open text bubbles with links to the relevant paper(s)
