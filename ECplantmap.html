An interactive map of literature studies of Uniola paniculata and Ammophila breviligulata in the region of overlapping range

This map accompanies a paper that is currently in review.

Orange dots are locations of studies focusing on Ammophila breviligulata.

Blue dots are locations of studies focusing on Uniola paniculata.

Purple dots are locations where both are studied.

The map is interactive, you can zoom, pan, and click markers. Clicked markers will open bubbles with links to the relevant paper(s) (via DOI and other stable URLs).

The dat underlying this map was collected by many people: From Oregon State) — Sally Hacker, Katya Jay, and Rebecca Mostow (OSU); From VCU — Joeseph Brown and Julie Zinnert; From UNC — Rueben Biel, Evan Goldstein, Laura Moore, and Elsemarie Mullins
